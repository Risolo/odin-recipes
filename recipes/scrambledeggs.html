Scrambled Eggs

Whisk THIS up for the kids this Sunday!

Eggs are my absolute favorite way to get my day going. I always try to start off with these because they give you the protein you need while also being the infertile embryo of another species!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Heat
  • Butter

Step-by-step Guide

  1. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl
  2. Whisk for 2 minutes
  3. Apply salt and pepper to the eggs
  4. Apply cheese to the eggs
  5. Put butter into the hot pan
  6. Pour in the eggs
  7. Gently stir the eggs in the pan until done (about 5 mins)
  8. Eat
